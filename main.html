Jaipur

Jaipur, also called the Pink City, is one of the most famous tourist destinations in India. Known for its rich history, grand palaces, forts, and vibrant bazaars, the city perfectly blends heritage with modernity. Popular attractions include the Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar (a UNESCO World Heritage site). Walking through the colorful streets of Jaipur feels like stepping back in time, surrounded by stunning architecture and lively culture.
